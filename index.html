The Property

The World's Most
Prestigious Address

Constructed in 1877 under the reign of King Victor Emmanuel II, Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II didn't just set the standard for luxury retail — it invented it. Stretching between the Milan Duomo and La Scala Opera House, no other retail address on earth commands this cultural gravity.

Today, it is not merely a shopping destination. It is a living monument — a UNESCO-recognized heritage site where 15 million visitors from 180 countries come annually, not just to shop, but to experience the apex of Italian civilization.
